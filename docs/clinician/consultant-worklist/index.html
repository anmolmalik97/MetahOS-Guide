Clinician Worklist

Once you login as a Doctor/Clinician/Consultant/Nurse/PRE/Doctor Secretary You will see the worklist which is your landing page

Calendar viewâ€‹

Worklist can also be accessed in a calendar view. Which can be further drilled down into a monthly daily or weekly view.

Actionsâ€‹

On the Worklist you can perform multiple actions

Searchâ€‹

  1. Patient can be searched using Partial (Name,Mobile or UHID)

Patient Nameâ€‹

  1. Clicking on the Patient Name will take you the Patient Longitudinal Record

Current Statusâ€‹

  1. Modifying the Current status of the patient will help you manage the patients queue (The PRE/Secretary can do a manual check-in, or the patient can self check-in when they enter the department)

Eg - system will change the color of worklist line item as per the status of the consultation

Consultâ€‹

  1. Click on Consult to start the consutlation ( Available only to Clinicians )

Assessmentsâ€‹

  1. Click on Assessments to run in-person or remote assessments (Depending on the department the Nurses/Clinicians can run various assessments for example: Antenatal Assessment which is typically used in OBG/GYN)

Edit/Referâ€‹

  1. Click on Edit/Refer to Edit the Consultation or Refer the patient (The PRE/Secretary can Reschedule the Consultation or Assign it to different Speciality/Provider and wherever required Refer the Patient to another Consultant based on the Consultant's Advise)

Vitalsâ€‹

  1. Click on Vitals to log Vitals for the Patient (The Nurse Can Log Vitals for the Patient)

Additonal Actionsâ€‹

  1. To Perform Additional Actions Click on the ... to view the menu here

Callâ€‹

  1. You can click on the Call button to Call the Patient

Print Rxâ€‹

  1. Print Rx will print the Prescription Case Notes for handwritten consultations with Prefilled Doctor and Patient Details

Upload Rxâ€‹

  1. Upload Rx is used to upload hand written consultations by the PRE/Secretary

Uploaded filess can then be viewed using the prescription button

Print Barcodeâ€‹

  1. Barcode button on OPD Worklist is used to print any numbers of barcodes.

Vaccinationâ€‹

  1. Vaccination button is used to log any Vaccinations that are administered by the Clinician or to log a past vaccination

Unschedulingâ€‹

  1. Unschedule button to Unschedule a consultation, this is used by the PRE/Secretary to unschedule a consultation by providing a reason

Completionâ€‹

  1. Complete Button will complete the consultation without entering the details.

Typically The Clinician/Consultant will click on Consult button to start entering consultation notes.

Edit Completed Consultationâ€‹

  1. Edit Button in completed worklist will let clinicians to edit a completed consultation. The system by default logs all modifications in a completed consultation and maintains an Audit Trail.
