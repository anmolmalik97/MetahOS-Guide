Voucher Management

Voucher management in a system refers to the process of financial transactions in organizations.

Filter Options-

Vendor Name: This section lists all the vendors. User can view and select vendors from various categories, facilitating the management and distribution of vouchers to the appropriate recipients.

Voucher Type Filter: This filter helps users sort and view vouchers based on their type. There are three types of filters available:

  • Journal: Vouchers related to journal entries, typically used for internal accounting adjustments.
  • Payment: Vouchers that record payments made to vendors or other parties.
  • Receipt: Vouchers documenting received payments or receipts from customers or clients.

Features-

By clicking on New Voucher Entry-

The page will feature the following options:
A. New Voucher Entry: This section allows users to create a new voucher. Users can input the necessary details to generate a voucher, specifying the value, purpose, and relevant terms and conditions.

  1. Journal Voucher Type
  • Choose “Journal” as your voucher type.
  • Reference Number: It will be automatically generated.
  • Voucher Date: Select the date associated with the voucher transaction.
  • Store: Choose the store for this voucher.
  • Journal No-It will be automatically generated. User can edit it if needed.

NOTE: The debits must be equal to the credits for the transaction to be submitted.

  1. Payment Voucher Type

  • Choose “Payment” as your voucher type.

  • Select the bank account you’ll use.

  • Select Payment Type (General).

  • Enter the recipient’s information (ledger account).

  • Input the payment amount.

    Use “Add Row” to include additional ledger.

  • General Payment -the transfer of monetary value from one party to another, either in cash or noncash form.

By selecting the Payment type (Advance)-
An advance payment is made before goods or services are delivered.
The user needs to provide the vendor’s name, requisition number, amount, and description.

Note: Upon selecting the vendor’s name, Requisition numbers are available for the specific vendor, the user can select the appropriate one, and the amount for that requisition number will be automatically filled.

  1. Receipt Voucher Type
  • Select “Receipt” as your voucher type.
  • Choose the bank account where the funds are being deposited.

  • Once a user has created the voucher successfully, user will see a confirmation message on your screen.

  • Access your latest vouchers instantly on the voucher management homepage.

  • User can easily view the details of a voucher by clicking on the blue icon.

  • Edit the voucher details by clicking on the yellow icon.

  • Green icon to approve the voucher.

  • Red icon for rejecting the voucher.

  • By clicking on the yellow icon “Edit” pop-up will appear on the screen-

  • Here, the user can edit the details.

C. Bulk Voucher Entry


  • Select Voucher Type: Choose the type of voucher that matches your data - Journal, Payment, or Receipt.
  • Download Sample: For each type, a sample file is available for download. This sample shows the required format for your data.
  • Fill & Upload: Open the downloaded sample file, fill in the details for your vouchers according to the format, and then upload the completed file.

After uploading your file, you’ll see a screen where you can enter the following information: It can be different for different Voucher types(Payment or Receipt).

  • Store: Select the store this purchase applies to.
  • Date: Choose the date of the purchase.
  • Credit Account: Specify the account to be credited (typically the vendor).
  • Debit Account: Indicate the account to be debited (usually an expense account).
    Once you’ve entered this information, click the ‘Submit’ button to see all your voucher entries on the main screen.
  • Pending Status: All newly created entries will have a “Pending” status until you review and approve them.

D. Bulk Approve or Reject

User can bulk approval or rejection of pending vouchers by selecting them from a list.

When the user selects vouchers from the list and chooses “Bulk Approve,” they can also provide a common remark for all approved vouchers.

After selecting vouchers from the list and choosing “Bulk Reject,” users can provide a common remark that will be applied to all rejected vouchers.
