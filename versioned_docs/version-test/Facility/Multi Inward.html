Multi Inward:

Facility>Inventory Management>Item Manager>Action>Multi Inward

  1. In the Action Section, select "Multi Inward."
  2. Choose the Store: Select the store where the incoming goods will be received.
  3. Select the Vendor: Choose the supplier who delivered the goods.
  4. Enter Invoice Number: Enter the invoice number provided by the supplier.
  5. Payment Status: Select whether the invoice for this delivery has been paid (Paid) or is still pending (Unpaid).
  6. Purchase Order (PO) Number: Enter the PO number associated with the order you want to receive goods for.
    Pre-populated Items: Once the user enters the PO number, the system will automatically populate the “Add Inward” section with the list of items included in that specific purchase order.

Note: - User can generate a PO number by clicking ‘Add PO Number’."

Review and Select Items: Review the list of items and select only the items you are receiving in this delivery. You can deselect any items not included in the current shipment.
7. Upload Invoice: Click “Upload Bill/Invoice” to attach a digital copy of the supplier’s invoice.
8. Add Inward: Click the “Add Inward” button.
9. Select Items: A list of items included in this delivery will appear. Choose the specific items user are receiving.
10. Fill in Item Details: For each selected item, you will need to enter the following information:
Batch: Enter the batch number assigned to this specific group of items.
Lot: Enter the lot details.
Expiry: Expiry date of product.
Package: Select the Package type.
MRP: Enter the manufacturer’s recommended selling price for the item.
Qty: Enter the quantity.
Free Cost: Enter any free goods received with this item (cost may be zero).
Total Sch Disc: Enter the total scheduled discount offered by the supplier.
Discount: Enter any additional discount received beyond the scheduled discount.
Discount Amt: The system will automatically calculate the total discount amount based on your entries.
Sub Total: The system will automatically calculate the subtotal for each item.
GST%: Enter the applicable Goods and Services Tax (GST) percentage.
GST Amt: The system will automatically calculate the GST amount based on the subtotal and GST percentage.
Grand Total: The system will automatically calculate the final grand total for each item.

11. Save All Inward: Once you’ve filled in the details for all received items, click “Save All Inward.”
