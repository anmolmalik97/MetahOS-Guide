FACILITY

Access Meta HOS:
Log in to your Meta HOS account using your credentials.

Navigate to Purchase Requisition Module:
Locate and select the “Purchase Requisition” module from the main menu. This module is typically found under the Facility.

The homepage will display options such as:

  • Refresh: Updates the page to display the most current information.
  • New Requisition: Allows users to create a new purchase request.
  • Purchase Log: Displays a record of all purchase transactions.
  • Work Log: Shows a record of all work activities and tasks.
  • Filter by Status: Enables users to sort entries based on their current status.

Filter the Status
Consist of:

  • Pending: Indicates that the request or task is awaiting approval or further action.
  • Approved: Indicates that the request or task has been authorized or accepted.
  • Rejected: Indicates that the request or task has been declined or denied.

Select the “Purchase Log” option.

This will provide a summary of the Approved Purchase Log, including any added Remarks.

Start New Requisition:
Click on the “New Requisition” button to initiate a new purchase requisition.

Fill in Requisition Details:


- Vendor Information:
- Enter the details of the vendor from whom you wish to make the purchase.
- This may include vendor name, contact information, and any relevant information.
- Item Details:
- Specify the items you need to purchase.
- This includes item descriptions, quantities required, unit prices, and any other details.
- Budget Allocation:
- Allocate the budget for the requisition, specifying the cost center or budget code to be charged for the purchase.

Review and Approval:
Review the requisition details to ensure accuracy and completeness.
Submit the requisition for approval.
The requisition may need approval from one or more stakeholders.

Monitor Status:
Keep track of the requisition’s status within the Meta HOS system.
You should be able to see whether it’s pending approval, approved, rejected, or in progress.

By Clicking Edit Option

A pop-up will appear asking if you want to edit.

Edit Purchase Requisition
Edit the details after submission by clicking on the edit option.

Receive Goods or Services:
Once the purchase requisition is approved and processed, the goods or services requested will be delivered.
Ensure that you receive the items in accordance with the requisition details.

  • Invoice will be generated.
