PAYMENT MANAGEMENT
FACILITY>PAYMENT MANAGEMENT
Navigate to payment management.

  • A home screen will appear where we can view-
    Sum Of Invoices, Sum Of Paid Invoices, Sum Of Unpaid Invoices, Sum Of Paid Amount, Sum Of Unpaid Amount

  • By Clicking on the setting button this screen will appear-

    User can manage groups and ledgers efficiently-
  1. Download Sample: Get a head start by downloading a sample sheet that includes the correct format for adding information.
  2. Fill in the Sample: Use the downloaded sample sheet as a template. Add your groups and ledger entries following the provided format.
  3. Upload Updated Sheet: Once you’ve finished adding your information to the sample sheet, simply upload the updated file back into the system.

* From here user can choose the types like groups and ledgers-

  • From here user can select the store-

  • By selecting this blue filter button from here-

  • This will show on screen-

  • From here user can filter out the Paid, Unpaid bills, and Partial paid-

  • From here user can select the Generated and Non generated bills-

  • From here user can view the filter type-

  • By selecting the calendar view-

  • This screen will appear-
  • The red color will show payment is pending.

  • The green color will show payment is successful.

  • User can view specific invoices by clicking on a particular date.

  • Clicking on the vendor name will display the payment details, including Invoice Information and payment status(Paid and Unpaid).

  • When the status shows as unpaid, click on Payment-

On this screen, user can choose a Ledger, Store/Branch, and a TDS rate. The TDS amount is adjustable. User have two options for applying TDS:

  • Apply the same TDS rate to all work items.
  • Set a separate TDS rate for each work item.

  • A successful notification will appear upon generating the payment advice.

  • After completion of the payment, an option “Generated” will appear, and clicking on the “Invoice” will display detailed invoice information.

  • The detailed invoice will be displayed in PDF format.

Download: Get a pre-formatted Excel sheet for your TDS data.
Fill it in: Easily enter details about deductions and payees.
Upload and Submit: Upload the completed sheet and click submit.
User TDS information will be updated.
