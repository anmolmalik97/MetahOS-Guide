PAYMENT WORKLIST

Navigate to the “Payment Worklist” within the “Bills” module.

The search option allows filtering by dates and vendors within the bills module, with the additional feature of a bank upload option.

The first option is for selecting the Order Type (Work or Purchase).

The second option is a Vendor Filter, which assists in Selecting the Vendor.

The third option is an Invoice Status Filter (Pending, Approved, Rejected).

The fourth option is a Payment Status Filter (Pending, Processing, Failed, Partially Paid, Successful).

PROCESSING PAYMENT FROM THE PAYMENT WORKLIST

Upon accessing the homepage, comprehensive payment details, including payment status and actionable items are seen.

Here we can see the Payment worklist Homepage, where both Payment Status and Approval Status can be tracked.

  • Payment status indicates whether a Payment is Paid, Pending, or Partially completed.
  • Approval Status shows the Approval Stage of the Payment.

Clicking on the Payment option

Triggers a pop-up to appear on the lower left side of the screen.

Clicking the arrow will display the total payable amount along with the next option.

Choose the mode of payment and then proceed by clicking "Complete Payment."

Once the details are verified, a pop-up will appear prompting you to click "Proceed to Payment."

A notification indicating successful completion of the payment will appear on the lower left side of the screen.

UPLOADING BANK DETAILS IN THE BANK UPLOAD OPTIONS

To initiate bank upload, select the “Bank Upload” option visible on the home screen.

Click “Payment” option.

Fill in the Account Details (such as Accounts Receivable, Accounts Payable, etc.), along with the UTR number.

The Payment Status can be marked as either Successful or Failed.

NOTE: If the Payment is marked as “Failed”, the Account Option Cannot be Selected. The Account Option can Only be Selected if the Payment is Marked as “Successful”.

Then Bank Upload Generated Sucessfully pop-up will appear.

To retrieve the Excel sheet, click on “Downloads” within the system.

The system will download an Excel sheet.
