Expense Management

It’s essentially the process of keeping your spending in check and making sure your money is being used efficiently.

This screen gives you complete control over tracking your organization’s spending. It displays current expenses for common categories like electricity, water, food, and any other expenses. You can easily add custom categories to track any additional expenses. This screen lets you switch between daily, weekly, monthly, and even yearly breakdowns of your expenses.

Track the status of your items: This area helps the user see if items are approved, rejected, or pending.

Selecting ‘Add another bill’ opens a menu where user can categorize their expenses as rent, transportation, etc.

To add a new expense entry, the User provides the following details:

  • Date: The date on which the expense was incurred.
  • Expense Type: The category of the expense, which can be one of the following:
    • Rent: Payment for housing or office space.
    • Transportation: Costs related to travel, such as fuel, public transit, or vehicle maintenance.
    • Food Allowance: Expenses for meals and groceries.
    • Utility: Payments for essential services like electricity, water, gas, or internet.
  • Expense Name: A brief description or name for the specific expense item.
  • Total Expense: The total amount of money spent on the expense.
  • Payment Status: Paid/Unpaid.
    Users can refine the expense list by selecting the type user want to see, like electricity, water, food, or others.

    By clicking the download button, users can obtain the expense list in an Excel format.

    User can easily add new expense categories by selecting ‘Add expense type’.

    The date filter specifies a start and end date, allowing you to view expenses incurred within that timeframe.
